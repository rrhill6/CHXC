News

Practice is underway! Please be sure to hydrate throughout the day, don't wait until you are at practice. Athletes will also need a watch and proper running attire. Coaches will be monitoring the heat index and practices will be adjusted according to the heat policy of DCSS.

Please be familiar with the rules and guidelines we have outlined in the parent packet. Athletes need to be picked up at the end of practice at our practice location. The practice locations are sent out via email on Sundays.
